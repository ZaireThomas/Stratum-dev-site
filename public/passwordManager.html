SecurePass is a simple offline password manager. You can safely store your credentials, encrypt them with a master password, and download them as a secure file. All on your own device.



Use the '+' button to add a new password entry, then type in a master password and hit the 'lock' button to encrypt your entries to a file. First entering your master password, dragging it into the box above.
